## Monster Break Order

<SpoilerBlock buttonLabel="Explanation">
    Legend:

    - (S): Breakable with sever damage only.
    - (B): Breakable with blunt damage only.

    *Bonus Break* is usually not possible, but I like trying to see if I can get a break anyway. In the table below, if I list nothing for Bonus Break, then there are no other breaks available. Just kill it.

    *Kill-Only* is what you'd focus on if you don't want to risk running the hunt timer, optimizing for best hitzone value with good accessibility. If I list nothing for *Kill-Only*, then it is implied that you can just focus on the first break part. This keeps the table simple.

    I tend to prefer starting with the tail since we also hit the head when the monster turns to maximize uptime anyway.
</SpoilerBlock>

Reminder: If you're short on a particular material, check the drop tables and adjust your break order.
