## Upgrade Potential

All rats get:

- You always get the initial upgrade, depending on the rat.
- You always get 30 more upgrades by endgame (one upgrade at the end of each wave).

Builds:

- Most rats:
    - You only get the 31 upgrades.
- Mr. Lucky: Start, Up, Up, Right, Up, Left
    - You get 6 more upgrades, for a total of 37 upgrades
