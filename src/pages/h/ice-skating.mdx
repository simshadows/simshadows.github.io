## Aussie Skate Program Handbook - References

*To help with my self-teaching, I used the Aussie Skate Program Handbook (found [here](https://aussieskate.au/)) as a reference for progression, and then I found some video resources explaining/demonstrating the techniques.*

**[Aussie Skate Program Handbook used for reference: 2022-06-02](https://aussieskate.au/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Aussie-Skate-Handbook_2022-Review_02.06.2022.pdf)**

### Aussie Skate 1

- **A. Stand Unassisted for 5 seconds**
- **B. Dip in place**
- **C. Correct way to fall and stand up on ice**
- **D. March Forward across the ice (8-10 steps)**
- **E. Forward 2-foot Glide (1 metre)**
- **F. Forward Swizzles (6-8 in a row)**
- **G. Backward Wiggles (6-8 in a row)**

- **H. Rocking Horse (3 in a row)**
- **I. Snow Plow Stop standing still**
- ~~J. Forward 1-foot Glide (R & L) *(2 seconds)*~~
- **K. Optional Element - Forward Dip whilst moving**

### Aussie Skate 2

- **A. Dip while Moving**
- **B. Backward Swizzles (6-8 in a row)**

- **D. Moving Snow Plow Stop**

### Aussie Skate 3

- **B. Forward 1-foot Glide (R & L)** *(3-4 seconds)*
- **C. Forward 1/2 Swizzle Pumps on a Circle CW & ACW (6-8 in a row)**
- **Optional Skill - Commence Forward Stroking**
