Sim's FFXIV Savage Mechanics Cheatsheet

This page is intended to be full of shorthand reminders on how savage raid mechanics work, for the experienced-but-forgetful among us. If you are new to a raid, watch a full guide first, then come back to this cheatsheet to remind yourself.

Currently an early work in progress. I will be working on figuring out how to make it better!

Common Abbreviations/Shorthand

  • "cardinal": North/South/East/West
    • "intercardinal": Between the cardinal directions (NE/NW/SE/SW).
  • LP: Light party
  • "cone AOE"/"pizza slice": Imagine the boss is in the middle of a pizza. A "pizza slice" AOE occurs in one slice.
  • "puddle": A small AOE that drops at players' feet and is dangerous to keep standing inside of. Dodge it after you drop it.

[P6S] Abyssos: The Sixth Circle (Savage)

My notes are only applicable for melee DPS.

Recurring mechanics:

  • [Exocleaver]: Dodge BOTH hits.
  • "Choros" [Choros Ixou]: Watch for TELEGRAPH. Dodge BOTH hits.
  • "Puddle" [Dark Dome]: Drops an INVISIBLE PUDDLE the moment the cast finishes.
  • "Cleaving Snake/Wing": Snake cleaves your front. Wing cleaves behind you.

Phases abstract:

  1. [Poly 1] Stay in LP stack. Dodge Exocleaver.
  2. [Pathogenic Cells] One-at-a-time pizza slice cleave from 1 to 8.
  3. [Exchange of Agonies 1]
    • Donut/stack marker IN, fireball OUT.
    • Tether means do the opposite.
    • Pay attention to "Choros".
  4. [Transmission] "Cleaving Snake/Wing" and "Choros".
  5. [Poly 2] Memorize safe spot. Drop and dodge "puddle". TODO
  6. [Exchange of Agonies 2]
    • Stack marker OUT, fireball IN.
    • Dodge Exocleaver.
  7. [Poly 3] Memorize safe spot. Stay in LP stack. TODO
  8. [Dark Ashes] Spread and dodge Choros Ixou.
  9. [Cachexia 1]
    • Bait order: 20 → 8 → 12 → 16
    • Bait the snake/wing OPPOSITE of your debuff icon.
    • Stay spread throughout whole phase (except for necessary movement).
    • Ptera Ixou: Go to the snake/wing side OPPOSITE of your new debuff icon.
  10. [Poly 4] Memorize safe spot. TODO
  11. (repeat Exchange of Agonies)
  12. [Poly 5]
    • Memorize safe spot. TODO
    • Drop and dodge "puddle".
  13. [Cachexia 2]
    • Go to the snake/wing side OPPOSITE of your new debuff icon.
    • Memorize safe spot. TODO
  14. [Poly 6] "Something diagonal to a corner-plus.
    • Drop and dodge "puddle".
  15. [Poly 7] Use only the "inner" safe squares.

[P7S] Abyssos: The Seventh Circle (Savage)

My notes are only applicable for melee DPS.

Recurring mechanics:

  • "Bough" [Bough of Attis]: Cleaves platforms.
  • [Forbidden Fruit]
    • "Circle/Bull Fruit" spawns:
      • "Bull" → Cleaves its platform.
      • "Tethered Bull" → Line AOEs.
    • "Lines/Bird Fruit" spawns:
      • "Bird" → Line AOE.
      • "Tethered Bird" → Line AOE WITH KNOCKBACK.
    • "Mouth/Minotaur Fruit" spawns:
      • "Minotaur" → Drops dodgeable cone AOEs. Targets two closest players.
      • "Tethered Bird" → You must stretch this. This does a cone AOE.

Phases abstract:

  1. [Blades of Attis] Everyone go to safe side.
  2. [Forbidden Fruit 1] Front platform WITHOUT "Bull Fruit" is safe. LP stacks.
  3. [Debuffs]
    • "Bough" → Back is safe. Arrows is unsafe.
    • Debuff numbering: 1 means do it first, 2 is second, etc.
    • "Red Debuff" → Spread
    • "Blue Debuff" → Stack
  4. [Forbidden Fruit 2] Spread and dodge "Birds". KNOCKBACK RESIST as soon as knockback indicator shows.
  5. [Forbidden Fruit 3]
    • Dodge the "Bulls" via. the bridge.
    • "Bough" → Back is safe. Arrows is unsafe.
  6. [Forbidden Fruit 4]
    • "Bull Tether" → Spread at bull platform.
    • "Minotaur Tether" → Stretch to opposite minotaur platform.
    • Untethered → Bait untethered minotaur.
  7. [Blades of Attis] Ride the knockback to SE/SW corners. Stay in LP stacks.
  8. [Forbidden Fruit 5] ("Birds and Towers")
    • "Bird Tether" → Go opposite.
    • Untethered → Towers. Sit towards the edge of the arena.
    • "Immortal's Obol" → Everyone go to one platform.
  9. [Inviolate Purgation (FF6)]
    • TODO
  10. [Forbidden Fruit 7]
    • Start at far edge of empty platform.
    • When chasing AOEs show, dodge via. bird that isn't facing you.
  11. [Famine's Harvest]
    • Start at 2-fruit platform.
    • TODO
  12. [Death's Harvest]
    • "Bull Tether" Spread at your appropriate platform.
  13. [War's Harvest]
    • "Bull Tether" → Spread at bull platform.
    • "Bird Tether" → TODO.
    • Both "Minotaur Tethers" → TODO.
  14. Easy Mechanics
    • An LP stack will appear.
    • "Bough" → Back is safe. Arrows is unsafe.
