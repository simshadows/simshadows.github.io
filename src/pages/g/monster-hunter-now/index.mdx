## Builds

I'm only playing through the game casually and lazily, so my builds reflect it.

I started playing the game shortly after launch and chose SnS. I didn't realize until too late that it's actually quite a difficult weapon to deal competitive damage. I'll be exploring moving to new weapons, but I already have reasonable SnS builds so I'll just focus on making do with what I have for now.

### Current Builds

These are very old meta. As in *launch year* old. I never bothered moving to new builds because the grind is just too much and I practically quit the game for a long time.

My plan will be to just max out the weapons and then these builds will mostly be "finished". They might still be used as my "lazy builds", e.g. when I'm walking around the street and just want to lazily beat up an 8* monster without looking at the screen too much. I might refine them a bit for this lazy purpose, but otherwise, these builds are "finished".

The special gauge build is a permanent build for quickly farming small monsters to recharge special gauge.
