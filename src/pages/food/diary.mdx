I try to make this list as complete as possible, but many stores/items/details may be missing for one of the following reasons:

- I might not remember the exact store/item/detail. (Example: This often happens if I'm just buying a common coffee from the nearest café.)
- It may be impractical to list the store/item/detail. (Example: Listing out specific ingredients from hotpot, or specific skewers from fried skewer shops.)

I sometimes try to put in diacritics/accents or native language characters, but I don't speak these languages so I may get it wrong when I do.
